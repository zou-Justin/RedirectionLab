iencescay! uetray aughterday ofhay oldhay imetay outhay arthay!
owhay alteresthay allhay ingsthay ithway ythay eeringpay eyeshay.
ywhay eyestpray outhay usthay uponhay ethay oet'spay earthay,
ulturevay, osewhay ingsway arehay ullday ealitiesray?
owhay ouldshay ehay ovelay eethay? orhay owhay eemday eethay iseway,
owhay ouldstway otnay eavelay imhay inhay ishay anderingway
otay eeksay orfay easuretray inhay ethay ewelledjay iesskay,
albeithay ehay oaredsay ithway anhay undauntedhay ingway?
asthay outhay otnay aggeddray ianaday omfray erhay arcay,
andhay ivendray ethay amadryadhay omfray ethay oodway
otay eeksay ahay eltershay inhay omesay appierhay arstay?
asthay outhay otnay orntay ethay aiadnay omfray erhay oodflay,
ethay elfinhay omfray ethay eengray assgray, andhay omfray emay
ethay ummersay eamdray eneathbay ethay amarindtay eetray?
